NJ Spotlight News covered the launch of OPRAmachine, highlighting how the platform democratizes access to government records across New Jersey's 565 municipalities and numerous state agencies.

**Article Highlights:**

The piece explored how OPRAmachine addresses common challenges citizens face when requesting public records:

- **Simplification**: Eliminates need to find correct custodian contact information
- **Tracking**: Automated follow-ups for overdue requests
- **Transparency**: All released records publicly searchable
- **Efficiency**: Prevents duplicate requests through shared database

**Expert Quotes:**

Good government advocates praised the platform as a significant step forward for transparency in New Jersey. The article noted that similar systems in other states have led to increased civic engagement and more informed public discourse.

**Impact Metrics:**

Within the first six months of operation, OPRAmachine processed thousands of requests covering topics from municipal budgets to police records, demonstrating strong public demand for accessible government information.

The coverage helped establish OPRAmachine as a trusted resource for journalists, researchers, and citizens seeking government accountability.

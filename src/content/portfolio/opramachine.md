## Overview

OPRAmachine is New Jersey's first statewide freedom of information platform, designed to make government more transparent and accountable. The platform automates the process of submitting Open Public Records Act (OPRA) requests to New Jersey government agencies, tracking responses, and publishing the results publicly.

## Impact

- **75,000+ requests processed** across hundreds of New Jersey government agencies
- **250GB+ of government data** released and made publicly accessible
- **565 government agencies** integrated into the platform
- Used by journalists, researchers, advocates, and everyday citizens

## Technology

Built on open-source infrastructure with a focus on accessibility, security, and scalability. The platform handles the entire lifecycle of public records requests, from submission through fulfillment and public disclosure.

## Recognition

OPRAmachine has been featured in numerous media outlets and has become an essential tool for government accountability in New Jersey. The platform demonstrates how civic technology can bridge the gap between citizens and government, making democracy more accessible.

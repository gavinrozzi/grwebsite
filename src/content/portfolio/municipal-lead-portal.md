## Overview

The Municipal Lead Reporting Portal is a statewide compliance platform designed to capture residential lead-paint inspection data from all 564 New Jersey municipalities. This critical public health infrastructure supports the enforcement of New Jersey's Lead Paint Inspection Law.

## Impact

**Operationalized NJ's Lead Paint Inspection Law**

The platform enables:
- Systematic tracking of lead paint inspections across the state
- Data-driven public health policy decisions
- Compliance monitoring for all municipalities
- Geographic analysis of lead exposure risks

## Key Features

- **Statewide Data Collection**: Unified system for all 564 municipalities
- **GIS Integration**: Geographic visualization of inspection data
- **Compliance Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of municipal reporting
- **Data Quality Controls**: Validation and verification systems
- **Secure Infrastructure**: Government-grade security and privacy

## Technology Stack

- **Enterprise Web Platform**: Scalable architecture for statewide use
- **GIS Integration**: Mapping and spatial analysis capabilities
- **Database Management**: Robust data storage and retrieval
- **Compliance Reporting**: Automated tracking and alerts
- **Secure Access**: Role-based permissions and authentication

## Public Health Impact

This platform plays a vital role in protecting children and families from lead exposure by:
- Enabling systematic tracking of lead hazards
- Supporting targeted intervention programs
- Providing data for policy development
- Facilitating coordination between state and local governments

## Recognition

The portal demonstrates how modern technology can operationalize complex regulatory requirements and support critical public health initiatives. It serves as a model for statewide compliance systems that balance usability with comprehensive data collection.
